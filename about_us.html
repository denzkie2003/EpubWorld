What is Freenovels?

Freenovels is lightweight website where anyone can download their favorite or newest translated novels from various sites. Freenovels support only Epub and Pdf file format to be downloaded and also accepting request of your novels to be made as epub or pdf.We only accepting request of novels from the listed translators. If your novels is not from this translators, feel free to send the link and we were do the rest.
